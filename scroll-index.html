Scroll Animation Prototypes

Pick a scroll behavior.

Ten prototype pages, each demonstrating a different scroll-triggered animation pattern as you move from section to section. Open them in tabs and compare.

01

Fade-in

Sections fade from transparent as they enter the viewport. The quietest option — almost subliminal.
02

Slide-up + fade

Each section rises ~40px from below while fading in. The classic, most-used pattern on modern sites.
03

Stagger reveal

Child elements (headings, paragraphs, cards) animate in sequence within each section.
04

Parallax

Background drifts slower than foreground content, creating depth as you scroll.
05

Sticky stack

Sections stick at the top while the next scrolls over them — pages feel like layered cards.
06

Scale-in

Sections start slightly smaller (0.95×) and grow to full size + opacity as they enter view.
07

Alternating slide

Odd sections slide in from the left, even ones from the right — gives a rhythmic side-to-side feel.
08

Clip-path wipe

Sections reveal via a top-down clip-path mask, like a curtain rising.
09

Blur to focus

Sections start blurred and sharpen into focus as they enter view. Cinematic.
10

3D rotate

Sections rotate slightly on the X axis as they enter, like pages flipping toward the viewer.
